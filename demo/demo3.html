Demo 3

Dragging objects under transformation (scale, rotation).

  1. Basic rectangle (no transformation); just for reference
  2. Tilter rectangle (scale and rotation); should follow a drag
  3. Group (scale and rotation); does not follow a drag
  4. Nested SVG (scale and rotation); does not observe the transformations; does follow a drag
  5. Symbol + use (scale and rotation); does follow a drag
This demo is used for testing different aspects of grouping elements together, while keeping them draggable under transformation. We only really need one such mechanism to work well (symbol + use looks most appropriate).
